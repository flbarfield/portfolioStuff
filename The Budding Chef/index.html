The Recipe Finder

If you've ever looked within your fridge and groaned at the idea of eating the same exact thing for the fifthteenth time in a row, try this tool instead! With it, you can enter any main ingredient and with the a quick search of our 143 dish databases, you'll get a slew of meal options to choose from!

If you're new to the site, give it a try! Go ahead and type "chicken" for a quick example in the left hand box! Or just leave it blank to see all available recipes! Once you have the name of the recipe, input that into the right box, and you'll have your directions!
