The Recipe Finder

If you've ever looked within your fridge and groaned at the idea of eating the same exact thing for the fifthteenth time in a row, try this tool instead! With it, you can enter any main ingredient and with the a quick search of our databases, you'll get a slew of meal options to choose from!

If you're new to the site, give it a try! Go ahead and type "chicken" for a quick example!
